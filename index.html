Why I Started This

A small shift in presentation can change a whole career

Hi, I'm Virendra Shivarkar.

As a QA professional with years of experience in software testing and enterprise applications, I've interacted with many talented individuals who struggled to get interview calls despite having strong technical skills.

I realized that the problem wasn't their knowledge — it was how they presented their experience.

  • ✕ Resumes that fail ATS screening
  • ✕ Resumes that lack relevant keywords
  • ✕ Resumes that don't highlight achievements
  • ✕ Resumes that miss recruiter expectations

That's why I started helping professionals improve their resumes and online profiles so their skills get the visibility they deserve.
